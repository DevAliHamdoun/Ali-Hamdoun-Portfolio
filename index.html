About Me

I’m a UX/UI Designer based in Beirut, focused on creating clean, accessible, and intuitive digital experiences. I believe good design makes complex tasks feel effortless.

I recently completed a Professional UX/UI Diploma at AUB, where I designed real-world projects including e-commerce platforms, responsive websites, and mobile applications. My process centers on user research, clarity, and practical design decisions.

I’m also building a stronger technical foundation in full-stack web development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and backend fundamentals) to better collaborate with developers and bring ideas to life more efficiently.

I love solving problems through thoughtful design and I’m always excited to work on projects that improve everyday experiences.
